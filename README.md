Project Title

Credit Risk Analysis and Loan Decision Dashboard

Project Overview

This project analyzes customer credit risk using Python for exploratory data analysis and Power BI for visualization. The objective is to support data driven loan approval decisions.

It helps identify high risk customers and enables financial institutions to reduce default risk while maintaining business growth.

Tools and Technologies

Python using Pandas NumPy Matplotlib and Seaborn
Power BI for dashboard development
DAX for calculated metrics and KPIs

Python EDA

Performed exploratory data analysis to understand patterns influencing credit risk.

Key analysis
Distribution of customers across risk categories
Relationship between loan amount and risk level
Impact of income on default probability
Correlation between financial variables

Key findings
High risk customers tend to take larger loan amounts
Lower income customers have higher default probability
Loan amount and income are major factors affecting risk

Power BI Dashboard

Developed an interactive dashboard to support loan decision making.

Key features
KPI metrics such as default rate risk distribution and rejection percentage
Risk segmentation based on loan amount income and age
Decision categories such as approve review and reject
Drill through feature for customer level analysis
Interactive filters for dynamic insights

Business Insights

High risk customers show significantly higher default rates
Credit risk is concentrated in low income and high loan segments
A portion of customers are rejected to reduce financial risk

Customer Level Analysis
Recommendations

Restrict high loan amounts for low income customers
Apply stricter approval rules for high risk customers
Focus on medium risk customers for balanced growth
